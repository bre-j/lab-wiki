<i> We present a generic framework for permutation inference for complex general linear models (GLMs) when the errors are exchangeable and/or have a symmetric distribution, and show that, even in the presence of nuisance effects, these permutation inferences are powerful while providing excellent control of false positives in a wide range of common and relevant imaging research scenarios </i> (Winkler et al., 2014) [@winklerPermutationInferenceGeneral2014a].
